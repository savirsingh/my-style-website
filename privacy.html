Privacy Notice


Hello, welcome to my small share of the World Wide Web. I'm glad you're taking some time to have a look at it. You were most likely redirected here by a contact/reservation form or cookie notice. Please take your time to read through this privacy notice in order to understand how I handle the information I receive.

I've divided this notice into 3 main parts:
- Contact/Reservation Forms
- Emails You Send Me
- Other Information I Collect

Contact/Reservation Forms
This website is hosted by GitHub, and therefore I am unable to receive messages through forms without using a third-party service. I currently use Thatformworks, which handles the backend for my forms. It essentially sends me messages via email. Each time you submit a contact/reservation form, I generally record your email, your message, and/or any other required field for at least 12 months. I use this information to reply to messages and to keep a record of every message I have received.

Emails You Send Me
I keep a copy of every email I receive (kopichiki@gmail.com) for at least 12 months. This is only to ensure that I am able to reply to your message with great detail and in order to keep a record of all messages I have received.

Other Information I Collect
I may collect additional information (like usage data, device information, location, etc.) in order to understand my audience better and to update this website whenever the need may arise.

I hope this notice well summarizes my use of your information. If you have any additional questions, please do not hesitate to ask me at kopichiki@gmail.com.
